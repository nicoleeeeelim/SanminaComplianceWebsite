About Our Quality Assurance Department

The Sanmina Quality Assurance (QA) department is at the forefront of ensuring product excellence and operational integrity. Our mission is to integrate robust quality management systems and foster a culture of continuous improvement across all stages of manufacturing and service delivery.

Our Department Structure:

We are committed to maintaining strict adherence to industry regulations, internal policies, and customer requirements. Through comprehensive compliance training, we equip our team members with the knowledge and tools necessary to identify, mitigate, and prevent quality deviations, ensuring every product meets the highest global standards.

Our Compliance Training Modules

ISO 9001:2015 Standards

In-depth training on the principles and requirements of ISO 9001:2015, focusing on quality management systems and continuous improvement processes. This includes coordination and preparation of materials for compliance and ethics training.

Regulatory Compliance (e.g., FDA, RoHS, REACH)

Understanding and adhering to specific industry regulations relevant to our products and markets, ensuring legal and ethical operations. This training ensures compliance with regulatory requirements to achieve high standards of quality.

Internal Audit Procedures

Training on conducting effective internal audits to assess compliance, identify non-conformities, and drive corrective actions. Uncertified training records can result in non-conformity and impact manufacturer certification.

Risk Management in QA

Techniques and methodologies for identifying, assessing, and mitigating quality-related risks throughout the product lifecycle. We review and update training matrices regularly.

Supplier Quality Management

Ensuring quality and compliance across our supply chain through robust supplier evaluation, monitoring, and development programs. We also participate in various projects such as revamping technical components of company orientation slides.

Data Integrity and Documentation

Best practices for maintaining accurate, complete, and consistent data records essential for compliance and traceability. This includes ensuring training records meet GDP compliance and timely filing.

MDSAP: Medical Device Single Audit Program

Our compliance training prepares us for yearly MDSAP audits, ensuring employees are trained and compliant with regulatory requirements. This program is crucial for achieving high standards of quality and maintaining manufacturer certification and market access.

Innovation & Efficiency: Our AI Project

We successfully implemented a Work Improvement Project focused on automating data filtering and chart generation using AI, specifically programmed with "App Script" and deployed via "Gemini".

Project Details:

  • Time Savings: Reduced the time experienced trainers spend on filtering data and producing bar charts from up to 2 hours down to just 5 minutes.
  • Simplified Job Scope: Streamlined the process, making tasks more efficient.
  • Cost Reduction: Contributed to cutting operational costs.

This project was presented in a staff communication session (highlighting new projects & KPIs) and was eligible for a Lean Six Sigma (LSS) conventional project.

The code's accuracy was verified through manual filtering, ensuring reliability.

Challenges & Solutions:

  • Challenge: Decoding code and generating automation of charts.
    Solution: Extensive research, understanding of code, and leveraging AI assistance. For chart automation, we utilized other applications.
  • Challenge: Certifying training records requires constant detail and can be draining.
    Solution: Implementing regular breaks and utilizing post-it notes for meticulous double-checking.
